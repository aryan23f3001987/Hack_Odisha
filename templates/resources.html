Quick Farming Tips

💡 Tip 1: Test your soil pH regularly - most crops thrive in slightly acidic to neutral soil (pH 6.0-7.0).


💡 Tip 2: Water early in the morning to reduce evaporation and prevent fungal diseases.


💡 Tip 3: Rotate your crops each season to prevent soil depletion and reduce pest buildup.


💡 Tip 4: Use companion planting - grow basil with tomatoes, or plant marigolds to deter pests.


💡 Tip 5: Mulch around plants to retain moisture, suppress weeds, and regulate soil temperature.
